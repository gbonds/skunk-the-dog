About

Hi! My name is Skunk and I am a smol dog. I’m about 4 years old but will always be a puppy at heart. I’m suspected to be a mix of Cocker Spaniel and Chihuahua, and perhaps just a little bit of cat.

I enjoy cuddling, parties, and generally being adored. I can strategically insert myself into the middle of any social interaction and especially enjoy sitting on laps. Besides my humans, I also love my tiny, tiny tennis balls and plush toys.
